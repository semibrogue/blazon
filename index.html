Blazon is an interactive quiz game: read a heraldic blazon and choose the matching shield, with bonus ranks up to Earl Marshal, gorged coronets, griffins, quarterly and per-bend fields, bezanty fields, ermine furs, a daily challenge, three shield styles (enamel, painted, engraved), and additive phase music.

Blazon

Read the blazon, then choose the shield whose beast matches. Rise from page to king of arms — and beyond, to Garter and the Earl Marshal — through guardant and reguardant heads, ordinaries and roundels, counterchanged fields, ornate lines of partition, forked and knotted tails, heads erased and couped, paired beasts, griffins and wyverns, coronets, furs of ermine and vair, and fields sown with bezants. Three misreadings end your career; fifty true readings earn you the accolade.

Devised by Jose de los Reyes
